Career Summary

Paulo Fidalgo is a distinguished software engineer holding a Master's degree in Computer Science, proficient in Ruby, Rails, and Java technologies. With over 15 years of experience in the software industry, more than a decade of which was spent working remotely, Paulo has a proven track record of success. He began his career in the Telco market, contributing to the development of backend systems for SMS-Centres, real-time communications (audio, video, and text) integrations. Subsequently, Paulo transitioned to the startup scene, leading a team in Big Data and Natural Language Processing, designing call routing systems to provide integrated customer support, and enabling wealth managers to unlock the full potential of Research Reports, Events, and Forecast Models. Paulo's latest venture as a founding engineer of an event tech company saw him facing technical challenges of hypergrowth, explosive customer demand, and scalability issues, further sharpening his skills and expertise.
